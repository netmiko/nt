What is McAfee Antivirus

McAfee Antivirus is a computer antivirus software that we use in our computer. When a virus enters our computer or some malware enters we can remove that virus from our computer system with the help of McAfee antivirus and make our computer more safe and secure.

To understand what a Antivirus is, we first have to know what a computer virus or malware is and how it can damage your computer and leak your private data.

What is a Computer Virus

Computer virus is a malicious code that can enter our computer system in any way and make it unusable or leak our data. When a computer virus enters your computer, it starts replicating itself. 

This means that it starts making duplicates of itself, due to which thousands of files start being created in your computer and due to which it is not possible to manually delete it from your system.

Which means that if a virus ever enters your computer, then you cannot run your computer properly because it will automatically start doing different things in your computer, which can damage your computer.

There are many different types of computer viruses in the world, which can do many different things by entering a computer, such as there are some computer viruses that after entering your computer starts sending email to all your contacts. 

There are also some computer viruses that make many copies of themselves, so that the storage of your computer’s hard drive becomes full, so that you cannot use it.

What is an Antivirus

An antivirus is a software that helps us to remove any virus or malware from our computer system.

That is, if a virus ever enters your computer, you can remove that virus by installing an antivirus.

Antivirus eliminates the virus from your computer and prevents it from ever entering in the future. As long as the antivirus is installed in your computer, the chances of any virus entering your computer are very less because it warns you in advance that a file may contain a virus, which alerts you. Before installing any file.

How does McAfee works

Once you have downloaded McAfee antivirus and installed it on your computer, it starts scanning the hard drive in your computer for any potentially harmful files and it will let you know if any virus remains in your computer. That this file may contain virus so that you can delete it with the help of McAfee antivirus.

McAfee Antivirus can detect and remove things like malware, spyware, browser hijacking from your computer. McAfee Antivirus is always on, that is, it always scans the files to be installed in your computer and informs you about any potential threats.

Features of McAfee Antivirus

McAfee Antivirus has many such features, with the help of which you can make your computer very secure and safe such as –

Email Scanning –

With the help of the email scanning feature, Mcafee antivirus scans any email coming to your computer so that if anyone have sent any malicious email to you that contains malicious code, it will block it so that you will know that it email is harmful to your device.

Email scanning is a very good feature because nowadays many such cases have been seen where people can send you malicious code in your email in any way, such as sending a virus to your computer with the help of an image. Or you can put that virus in your computer with the help of any text file or any document file, to avoid that we use the email scanning feature of Mcafee antivirus.

Malware Remove –

Malware is a malicious code that is designed to damage your computer, which means that if a malware enters your computer, it can do a lot of damage to your computer and make it unusable.

Mcafee antivirus has a very advanced feature of malware detection, with the help of which it detects any malware in your computer and removes it. If you are installing any new software, it scans it and warns you if any malware appears in any software.

Spyware detection –

Spyware is a program that is designed to spy on you, that is, if it enters your computer, it can hack your computer’s camera and hack your microphone and your computer’s camera. It can record whatever is happening in front.

This spyware records all the things in your computer and different things like what you are doing in your computer, what are you typing in your browser, what application are you using, what are you doing. To avoid this, we have to use any antivirus which detects spyware and removes it.

Webcam and microphone protection –

With the help of Mcafee antivirus, you can block your computer’s camera and its microphone from being used through any application. That is, no application can use your camera and microphone without your permission, which is a very high priority thing because someone can use them to spy on you, which can put your privacy and security at risk. .

Password manager –

If you want to keep the password safely stored in your computer, then you can use the password manager of Mcafee antivirus which keeps your passwords safely stored and which no one can access and see instead of you. . Because it is encrypted so that it is impossible to open it without a password.

But after that you have to buy a subscription plan for it if you want to use it more after 30 days is over. How to Install McAfee Antivirus

To install Mcafee antivirus in windows, you have to first open any web browser and in the search bar of that browser you have to type Mcafee free.

Now we have to click on the first result which will take us to the download page of mcafee antivirus from where you have to download its exe file by clicking on the download button.

After downloading the exe file in Windows, we will run that exe file and then it will take us to its setup wizard where we have to setup this software and after setting it up, it will be in your computer for 30 days now. It will be activated i.e. you will not have to pay any money for 30 days and it will take care of all the security for you and will scan for viruses.

You get everything in the free version of Mcafee antivirus. You get all the features of the paid version for 30 days in this free version, so you can easily download and use it and then if you like it then you can go to the paid version.

Mac –

To install Mcafee Antivirus on Mac, you have to first download its dmg file and then run it. After running it will ask you to setup which you will have to complete after which it will be installed in your mac which you can use now.

Android –

Mcafee antivirus software is not only limited to windows and mac, we can also use it in our android device for security. If you want to install Mcafee antivirus in android device then you have to first go to play store and search in play store.

After searching, Mcafee antivirus app will appear which you will have to install.

After installing, open this app, after opening it will ask you to accept the privacy policy which you will have to accept.

How to Uninstall McAfee

Windows –

It is very easy to uninstall McAfee antivirus from your windows computer. For this you have to first click on this link given below ” https://download.mcafee.com/molbin/iss-loc/SupportTools/MCPR/MCPR.exe “

Clicking on this link will download the MCPR tool in your windows which is what we need to uninstall Mcafee. With the help of this tool, it will remove all the Mcafee’s antivirus program files in your device.

After downloading this file, we have to run it, by running it its setup wizard will open where you will have to first click on agree and click on continue.

After clicking on continue, here you will have to solve a captcha. After doing all this it will start uninstalling Mcafee antivirus from your device.

It takes 5 to 10 minutes to uninstall it from your computer. Now we have to restart our computer once.

Linux –

To uninstall Mcafee antivirus from Linux, you have to first go to your application menu, here you have to select Ubuntu Software Center and then we have to search MAVL in the search box. Here we have to select McAfee Antivirus For Linux and then we have to click on remove.

Mac –

To install McAfee antivirus from Mac, you have to first go to the application where you have to double click on McAfee Internet Security Uninstaller.

After double clicking, you will have to click on continue, after which you will have to type your password in it, after which your Mcafee antivirus will be uninstalled.

If you face any problem in uninstalling, then you can go to the official website of Mcafee where you can get better help about it.

https://service.mcafee.com/ – By going here you can search for your problem and find the solution in its search bar.
